--- Scoring methodology ---

  Precision = TP / (TP + FP)
    Fraction of rule fires that are genuine matches.
    Low precision → false alarms erode analyst confidence.

  Recall = TP / (TP + FN)
    Fraction of genuinely malicious fixtures caught by the rule.
    Low recall → threats pass through undetected.

  F1 = 2 × Precision × Recall / (Precision + Recall)
    Harmonic mean. Treats a missed threat (FN) and a false alarm (FP) as equally costly.

  F2 = (1 + β²) × Precision × Recall / (β² × Precision + Recall)   [β = 2]
    β > 1: recall weighted more heavily. A missed threat is penalised 4× more than a false alarm.
    Rationale: in detection contexts a missed threat may allow an attack to proceed;
    a false alarm costs analyst time but does not allow the attack through.

  Publication requirement: Precision = 1.00, Recall = 1.00, F1 = 1.00, Fβ = 1.00
  Zero FP and zero FN are the only inputs that satisfy all four metrics simultaneously.
